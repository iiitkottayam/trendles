About Trendles

Trendles is one the clubs of IIIT Kottayam. The word 'Trendle' means wheel. At Trendles, we represent a wheel of duty of responsibility to streamline the club activities and to notify the students and other colleges about the events and activities pertaining to the institute so as to establish IIIT Kottayam in the social community”.

Trendles club also takes the responsibility in organising various personality building activities to boost the confidence, enrich and promote the student’s personality to develop themselves across various domains and interests.
